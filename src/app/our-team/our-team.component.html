Kathryn Steffy
Education:

J.D., American University Washington College of Law, 2019

M.A., Virginia Tech, 2016

B.S., B.A. Virginia Tech, 2015

Admissions:

Maryland

Washington, D.C.

Kathryn joined Metropolitan Legal Services with extensive experience in civil litigation and personal injury claims. Kathryn obtained her Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and a Bachelor of Science in Dairy Science from Virginia Tech in 2015. After completing her Master of Arts in Political Science from Virginia Tech in 2016, Kathryn began law school at Vermont Law School where she participated on the Emory Civil Rights and Liberties Moot Court Team, was a member of the Vermont Law Review, and was in the top 5% of her class. She completed her law degree from American University’s Washington College of Law and clerked in Judge Daniel Powell’s Chambers in Somerset County, Maryland. Katie joins our firm after working as an associate attorney at Downs Rachlin Martin, PLLC in Burlington, Vermont in their health law practice area, specializing in medical malpractice and wrongful death cases. In her spare time, Katie enjoys cheering on the Ohio State Buckeyes and spending time with her husband, Sam, and dog, Lucy.
